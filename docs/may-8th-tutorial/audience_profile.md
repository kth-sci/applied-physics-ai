## Framing Notes

- **Don't over-explain the basics** — 42% report regular use; 9% advanced. Skip "what is ChatGPT" and go straight to what changes with agents.
- **Lead with the human tension** — the PhDs/postdocs in the room worry about the job market; the Professors worry about how to supervise and teach. Both tensions are real.
- **Honour experimental intuition** — applied physicists have strong physical/domain intuition. Frame AI as amplifying that intuition, not replacing it.
- **Avoid jargon overload** — MCP, agentic loop, RAG etc. are fine to name once; don't dwell. The hands-on will teach more than the slides.
