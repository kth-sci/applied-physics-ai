## Notes & Assumptions

**Personnel**
- Rate of 6,000 SEK/day is an estimate (direct salary + ~60% overhead). Confirm with department finance.
- Time covers prep, material development, coordination, and delivery.
- Fredrik co-leads the Hackathon; his time is budgeted separately by SCI.

**AI Licenses**
- Pricing: Claude Team at $25/user/month (annual billing) ≈ 260 SEK/month at 1 USD = 10.5 SEK.
- All Claude Team licenses: 12-month annual subscription starting **July 1, 2026** (post-hackathon momentum; covers summer research period and the October workshop; runs through June 2027).
- Seats can be reassigned within the department when staff contracts end — no wasted cost on turnover.

**Non-permanent Pool (35 seats)**  
Pool is demand-allocated and administered by Wei and Jonas. Seat priority:

1. **PhD students** (49 eligible, strict count) — highest priority; largest group
2. **Postdocs** (29 eligible)
3. **Researchers** (27 eligible, strict "Researcher" title)
4. Remaining capacity for Stipendiats, Research Engineers, Visiting researchers, event participants

¹ With 35 seats and 105 eligible non-permanent staff, not everyone can be covered simultaneously. Seats should rotate with research activity and event schedules. A simple request process (email or short form) is recommended.

**Permanent Staff (35 seats)**
- Estimated headcount; confirm with HR before purchasing. Each seat is ±3,120 SEK/year.

**Events**
- Venue costs assume KTH internal rooms are free for Tutorial and Teachers' Meeting.
- Catering figures reflect Stockholm/KTH conference rates.
- Non-APHYS attendees at the Hackathon are expected to bring their own AI tool access or use trial accounts; they are not covered by this budget.

**KTH institutional agreements**
- KTH may provide Microsoft 365 Copilot to all staff. If so, drop Copilot from the Organizer bundle (saves 5,040 SEK) and verify if it covers any Claude Team needs.
